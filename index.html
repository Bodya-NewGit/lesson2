Geranium Tops World's 50 Best Restaurants 2022 - See the Full List

Geranium, by chef Rasmus Kofoed in Copenhagen, has been named number one on The World’s 50 Best Restaurants 2022 list. After coming second last year to fellow Copenhagen restaurant Noma , Kofoed this year finished top of the podium.The annual isting of The World’s 50 Best Restaurantsl was announced at an awards ceremony at London’s Old Billingsgate market, and hosted by Hollywood actor Stanley Tucci . As well as the top 50 countdown to the coveted title of the best restaurant in the world, there were also many special awards given out in addition to the usual awards that precede the big night.

A special thanks goes out to the most loyal and amazing team back in Copenhagen,said Kofoed. To my dear wife who is attending for the first time. For me, this recognition is not about being better than another, it’s a celebration of all of us.

Some special awards were announced during proceedings including the Sustainable Restaurant Award, which went to Aponiente in Cadiz, Spain. The Best Pastry Chefaward went to Rene Frank; the Gin Mare Art of Hospitality Award was given to Atomix; and the Beronia Word’s Best Sommelier Award, in its first year, went to Josep Roca.

As usual, several awards were announced in the lead-up to the event: Leonor Espinosa, of Leo restaurant, Bogotá, was the winner of The World's Best Female Chef 2022 award ; AM par Alexandre Mazzia in Marseille was named the American Express One To Watch 2022, and Kenyan humanitarian Wawira Njiru was recognised with the Icon Award . A new award, Champions of Change , recognised this year's heroes of gastronomy: educationalist Dieuveil Malonga, Ukrainian campaigners Olia Hercules and Alissa Timoshkina, and inspiring activist Koh Seng Choon.
